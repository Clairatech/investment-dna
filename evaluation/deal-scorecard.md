# Deal Scorecard

<!-- Scoring framework for incoming opportunities. Customize weights to reflect your firm's priorities. -->

## Scoring Dimensions

Rate each dimension 1-5 (1 = Poor, 5 = Exceptional). Multiply by weight for weighted score.

| Dimension | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **DNA Fit** | 20% | How well does this deal align with our core investment DNA? |
| **Criteria Match** | 15% | Does it pass our hard filters and meet our financial thresholds? |
| **Thesis Alignment** | 15% | Does it map to an active sector thesis? |
| **Team Quality** | 15% | Does the team meet our management criteria? |
| **Market Opportunity** | 10% | Is the market large, growing, and structurally attractive? |
| **Competitive Position** | 10% | Does the company have a defensible moat or path to one? |
| **Portfolio Fit** | 10% | Does it complement our current portfolio without excess concentration? |
| **Risk/Return** | 5% | Is the risk/return profile attractive relative to our framework? |

## Scoring Guide

### 5 — Exceptional
Clearly exceeds criteria. Would be a flagship example of what we look for.

### 4 — Strong
Meets all criteria with notable strengths. Minor gaps only.

### 3 — Adequate
Meets most criteria. Some concerns but nothing disqualifying.

### 2 — Below Standard
Misses on one or more important dimensions. Would need exceptional strength elsewhere.

### 1 — Poor Fit
Fundamentally misaligned with our DNA, criteria, or portfolio needs.
