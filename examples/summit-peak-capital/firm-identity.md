# Firm Identity

## Firm Overview

- **Firm name:** Summit Peak Capital
- **Founded:** 2018
- **Headquarters:** Austin, TX
- **AUM:** $420M across two funds
- **Fund structure:** Closed-end fund (10-year term + 2 one-year extensions)
- **Fund vintage/lifecycle:** Fund II, 2023 vintage, year 1 of deployment
- **LP base:** University endowments (35%), family offices (40%), fund of funds (25%)

## Mandate

- **Asset class:** Growth equity
- **Stage focus:** Series B through pre-IPO
- **Geography:** North America
- **Check size range:** $10M–$35M
- **Target ownership:** 8–15%
- **Target fund returns:** 3x net MOIC, 25%+ gross IRR
- **Target hold period:** 4–6 years

## Fund Economics

- **Management fee:** 2.0% on committed capital during investment period, 1.5% on invested capital thereafter
- **Carry/performance fee:** 20%
- **Hurdle rate:** 8% preferred return
- **GP commitment:** 3% of fund size

## Differentiators

- Deep operating bench — three full-time operating partners (ex-CRO, ex-CPO, ex-CFO of scaled SaaS companies) embedded with portfolio companies
- Proprietary data pipeline that screens 2,000+ SaaS companies monthly on product-led growth signals before they hit the fundraising circuit
- LP base that provides direct customer introductions to enterprise buyers (university systems, hospital networks, municipal governments)

## Constraints

- **Concentration limits:** No single position >12% of fund at cost
- **Geography restrictions:** North America only (US + Canada)
- **Sector exclusions:** No cannabis, no weapons, no fossil fuel extraction
- **ESG/impact requirements:** ESG screen at IC level; no formal impact mandate but LP-requested exclusion list
- **Co-investment obligations:** Offer co-invest to LPs on any deal >$25M
