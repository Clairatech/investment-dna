## Decision-Making Process

- **Sourcing:** All team members source. 40% from data pipeline (proprietary PLG screen), 30% from network/referrals, 20% from inbound, 10% from conferences/events. James runs the pipeline and does first-pass screening.
- **Initial screen:** James and one partner review within 48 hours of entry into pipeline. Kill or advance to deep dive. Need one partner sponsor to proceed.
- **Due diligence:** Partner sponsor leads DD with James supporting. 4-6 week process. Standard DD checklist + operating partner technical DD on product and GTM. Customer calls (minimum 8), competitor mapping, financial model build.
- **IC presentation:** 45-minute presentation + 30-minute debate. Required materials: investment memo, financial model, customer call summaries, competitive analysis, operating plan.
- **Approval process:** Unanimous among partners for deals >$20M. Majority (2 of 3) for deals ≤$20M. Managing Partners have veto rights on any deal.
- **Post-investment:** Partner sponsor takes board seat. Operating partner assigned within 90 days. James monitors KPIs monthly and flags deviations.

## Communication Cadence

- **IC meetings:** Weekly on Tuesdays, 2 hours. First hour: pipeline review and new opportunities. Second hour: portfolio updates or deep dives.
- **Portfolio reviews:** Full portfolio review quarterly (all-day session). Each company gets 20-minute update + discussion.
- **LP reporting:** Quarterly letters + annual meeting. ILPA-compliant reporting. Ad-hoc updates for material events.
- **Team standups/syncs:** Daily 15-minute standup (async on Slack if not in office). Friday afternoon team debrief on the week.

## Decision-Making Culture

- Debate-heavy, conviction-driven. We want genuine disagreement aired in IC, not consensus-seeking. The partner sponsor is expected to be the strongest advocate; others are expected to stress-test.
- "Pre-mortem" is a required section of every IC memo — the sponsor must articulate the most likely way the investment fails.
- Post-decision, we commit fully. No passive-aggressive second-guessing after the vote. If conditions change, bring it back to IC formally.
